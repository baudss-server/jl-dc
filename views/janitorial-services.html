About Our Janitorial Company

DelaCruzPH Corporation delivers dependable janitorial solutions for offices, clinics, retail spaces, and buildings. Our trained teams follow documented checklists and safety standards to keep your space consistently clean—every visit.

From nightly maintenance to deep cleans and move-ins/outs, we tailor your scope to schedule, budget, and SLAs—with transparent reporting and clear escalation paths.

Our Janitorial Services

Regular Office Cleaning

Dusting, mopping, trash handling, pantry reset, and shared-area disinfection.

Deep Cleaning

High-touch points, grout/edges, vents, and behind fixtures—thorough top-to-bottom clean.

Floor Care

Scrubbing, polishing, waxing; restore floors and extend their life.

Interior & Exterior

Glass partitions, windows, storefronts, and exterior touchpoints.

Eco-Friendly Options

Green products upon request for a safer workplace.

Flexible Scheduling

Only pay for hours worked, with clear SLAs and weekly reports.
